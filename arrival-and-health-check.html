Transportation to camp site

From Northern Station:

by taxi:

  1. 15 min
  2. cca. 60 MDL

by public transportation, BUS nr 172 (every 6 min):

  1. 29 min
  2. 3 MDL
  3. from Northern station to station “Str. Liviu Deleanu”, then walk for 4 min

From Central Station:

by taxi:

  1. 18 min
  2. 60 MDL

by public transportation (option 1)

  1. 3 MDL
  2. 25 min
  3. walk 9 min to station Tighina. From Str. Tighnia, take the BUS nr 185 (every 4 min) to station "Str Liviu Deleanu", then walk for 4 min

(option 2) by public transportation

  1. 2 MDL
  2. 30 min
  3. (walk to str. Ismail (UNIC) 330 m) from station UNIC, take trolley nr 22 (every 9 min), and stop at station “Str. Liviu Deleanu”, then walk for 4 min

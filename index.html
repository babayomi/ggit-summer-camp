About the summer camp

GirlsGoIT summer camp is a 10 days workshop programme. It starts from the 14 to 23 August. An exposure for you to learn in-demand skills.


Team building activities

Improve yourself, learn new skills and make new friends from around 20 regions from Moldova

Digital skills and STEM Workshops

Learn cutting-edge tools that powers the world of web app and 3D printing

Visit to tech companies

Gain more exposure, meet professionals and experts working in the tech industry

Internships opportunities

After completing your camp experience, you can be selected for internship

Our camp workshops are designed to teach you these skills

Essential soft skills & project managment

Improve your communication skills, learn how team works to build your final project.

Front-end web development & Data science

Build a project by learning web development and data science using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python

Product prototyping, 3D Printing & Electronics

Learn to design a product using 3D printing software and how 3D printer works from Atelier99

Build your leadership as an Ambassador of GirlsGoIT

Be part of the community! You can become an ambassador of GirlsGoIT in your communuity

Provide In-office or Remote Internships

Sponsoring GirlsGoIT requires your company to provide either in-office or remote internship for 1 or 2 months for participants who will graduate from GirlsGoIT summer camp. The participants will be trained on how to build their CV, How Team Works and Communication, Front-end web development, product prototyping and 3D printing. The role of the participants is to apply for an internship to your company during GirlsGoIT summer camp.

To provide internship, GirlsGoIT will engage your company in the following processes: Preparation for internship, Selection of Interns, On-boarding interns and Internship experience and evaluation. At this stage, we will start with preparing your company to provide internships.

Structure of internship

You can choose to provide in-office or remote internships. However works for your company and most convenient for your business operations and employees.

Internship duration and timeline

The standard in-office duration of internship is 1 month, and we recommend it start immediately after summer camp ends because participants must return to their schools. For remote internship, it is possible for more than 1-month duration (max 2 months), only if both parties (participants + your company) reach a common timeline agreement. We will also keep the parents of the participants aware. TEKEDU (implementing GirlsGoIT) will make sure of this.

Preparation for internship

  1. Preparation for internship, complete the summer internship form
  2. A member of TEKEDU team will contact you as soon as we receive your company's internship forms
  3. Sign an internship cooperation agreement with TEKEDU and assign an internship account manager
  4. Draft a press release about summer internship cooperation. Press release will be coordinated with your company
